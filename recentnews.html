Recent News About Cyberjaya

Autonomous Vehicle Testing

Cyberjaya is paving the way for the future with the first autonomous vehicle testing routes in Malaysia. Spearheaded by Futurise and the Ministry of Transport, this initiative within the National Regulatory Sandbox is set to accelerate the country's autonomous vehicle industry.

New MRT Station

Exciting developments are underway with the upcoming MRT station at Cyberjaya City Centre. This addition is poised to enhance connectivity, catering to the evolving transportation needs of our growing community.

Smart Traffic Management

Cyberjaya's streets are smarter than ever with the implementation of an AI-driven traffic management system. Utilizing IoT technologies, this system reduces congestion and enhances road safety by optimizing traffic flow in real-time.

Cyberjaya Innovation Hub

Welcome to the newly inaugurated Cyberjaya Innovation Hub! A collaborative space where entrepreneurs, researchers, and tech enthusiasts come together to push the boundaries of innovation. The hub offers state-of-the-art facilities to foster creativity and development.

Tech Festival 2024

Mark your calendars for Tech Festival 2024! Cyberjaya will host this grand event, bringing together industry leaders, startups, and tech enthusiasts from around the globe. Experience keynote speeches, panel discussions, and exhibitions showcasing cutting-edge technology.

Green Energy Initiatives

Cyberjaya is going green! New initiatives are underway, including the installation of solar panels city-wide and the introduction of electric public transportation options, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability and reducing our carbon footprint.
