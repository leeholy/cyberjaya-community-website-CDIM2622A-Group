Dell Cyberjaya

Located in the heart of Cyberjaya, Malaysia's technological powerhouse, Dell Cyberjaya is a strategically located facility for Dell Technologies. Dell has a number of departments operating out of Cyberjaya, including R&D, customer service, and administration. This facility plays a crucial role in Dell's global operations, leveraging Malaysia's highly skilled workforce and favorable business environment to drive innovation and customer service both locally and internationally.

Telekom Malaysia

Within Malaysia's technological cluster, Telekom Malaysia Berhad (TM) in Cyberjaya is a prominent supplier of digital services and telecommunications. With an emphasis on a range of telecommunications services, including broadband internet, fixed-line telephone, and digital solutions for both enterprises and consumers, Cyberjaya acts as the hub for TM's activities. By providing services and infrastructure essential to Malaysia's connectivity and communications requirements in the digital era, TM's presence in Cyberjaya highlights its role in assisting the country's digital transformation.
