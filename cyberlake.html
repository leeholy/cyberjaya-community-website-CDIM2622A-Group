Welcome to Cyberjaya Lake Garden

Beautiful Scenery at Cyberjaya Lake Garden!

The Cyberjaya Lake Gardens are a dynamic and important piece of nature located in the centre of Cyberjaya. Nestled among scrublands, secondary woods, and oil palm plantations, this 400-acre park provides an essential green space for the adjacent metropolitan population.
