Cyberjaya Lake Garden

What is Cyberjaya Lake Garden?

Cyberjaya Lake Garden is a beautiful recreational park in Cyberjaya. It features a large lake, jogging and cycling tracks, playgrounds, picnic areas, and lush green landscapes, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Where is Cyberjaya Lake Garden located?

Cyberjaya Lake Garden is located at Persiaran Semarak Api, Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia.

What are the operating hours of Cyberjaya Lake Garden?

The park is open daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Are there any facilities available at Cyberjaya Lake Garden?

Yes, the park offers various facilities including restrooms, a food court, fitness equipment, and a children's playground.

Are pets allowed at Cyberjaya Lake Garden?

Yes, pets are allowed in Cyberjaya Lake Garden as long as they are on a leash and owners clean up after them. It's a great place for a walk with your furry friends.

Tamarind Square

What is Tamarind Square?

Tamarind Square is a retail and lifestyle destination in Cyberjaya, designed to be a communal space where nature meets urban convenience. It features a mix of retail shops, offices, cafes, and restaurants, all set within a green, open-air environment.

Where is Tamarind Square located?

Tamarind Square is located at Persiaran Multimedia, Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia. It is easily accessible by car and public transportation.

What are the operating hours of Tamarind Square?

Most shops and eateries in Tamarind Square operate from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. However, specific operating hours may vary depending on individual tenants.

Is there parking available at Tamarind Square?

Yes, Tamarind Square offers ample parking spaces for visitors. There are both outdoor and basement parking options available.

Is Tamarind Square wheelchair accessible?

Yes, Tamarind Square is designed to be wheelchair accessible, with ramps and elevators available for easy navigation throughout the complex.

DPulze Shopping Center

What is DPulze?

DPulze is a popular shopping mall in Cyberjaya. It offers a wide range of retail stores, dining options, a cinema, entertainment outlets, and a hotel, making it a central hub for shopping and entertainment in the area.

Where is DPulze located?

DPulze is located at Lingkaran Cyber Point Timur, Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia.

What are the operating hours of DPulze?

The mall is open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

What can I find at DPulze?

DPulze features a variety of retail shops, a food court, restaurants, a cinema, entertainment outlets, and a hotel. It also hosts events and promotions throughout the year.

Is there a cinema at DPulze?

Yes, DPulze has a cinema operated by TGV Cinemas, offering a wide range of movies for entertainment. Check their website for showtimes and bookings.

Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya

What is Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya?

Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya is a trendy alleyway in Cyberjaya known for its vibrant street art, unique cafes, and indie shops. It is a popular spot for photography, social gatherings, and exploring local culture.

Where is Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya located?

Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya is situated behind some of the main commercial buildings in Cyberjaya. It is a hidden gem that is best discovered by exploring the area on foot.

What are the operating hours of Lorong Belakang Cyberjaya?

The cafes and shops in Lorong Belakang have varying operating hours, but most are open from late morning until late evening.
