Sekolah Seri Puteri, Cyberjaya

Sekolah Seri Puteri (SSP) in Cyberjaya is a prominent full-boarding school for girls situated in Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia. It stands out as one of the well-known Sekolah Berasrama Penuh (SBP) or Fully Residential Schools in the nation, overseen by the Ministry of Education Malaysia.

Phone: 03-8319 2414

Address: Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor

Cyberjaya Hospital

As part of the South Selangor Cluster Hospital Programme, Hospital Cyberjaya is a specialised hospital situated in Cyberjaya, Malaysia. It is a member of a wider network of hospitals that includes Hospital Serdang, Hospital Kajang, and Hospital Ampang. It is renowned for offering a variety of specialised medical treatments. The hospital offers cutting-edge medical services and facilities within its specialised domains in an effort to meet the healthcare requirements of the people in Cyberjaya and the neighbouring areas.

Hours: Open 24 hours

Phone: 03-8873 3500

Address: Persiaran Sepang, Cyber 11, 63000 Sepang, Selangor

Cyberjaya Fire Station

The main fire and rescue services facility for the Cyberjaya area of Malaysia is the Cyberjaya Fire Station, sometimes referred to as Balai Bomba Cyberjaya in Malay. Within its authority, it is responsible for reacting to situations like fires, accidents, and other calamities. The station is staffed by skilled individuals who are equipped to handle a variety of emergency scenarios, as well as rescue vehicles and firefighting equipment.

Hours: Open 24 hours

Phone: 03-8318 8944

Address: Persiaran APEC, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor

Cyberjaya Police Station

Located in Cyberjaya, Malaysia, is the Cyberjaya Police Department, a law enforcement organisation. Operating under the auspices of the Royal Malaysia Police (PDRM), it is in charge of upholding law and order in Cyberjaya, Malaysia's hub for digital innovation and information technology. Assuring public safety in the Cyberjaya region, preventing crime, conducting investigations, managing traffic, and other standard police force responsibilities are all handled by the department.

Hours: Open 24 hours

Phone: 03-8318 2222

Address: Jalan Cyber Point 5, 63000 Cyberjaya, Sepang, Selangor.

Raja Haji Fi Sabilillah Mosque

The main mosque in Cyberjaya, Sepang, Selangor, Malaysia is called the Raja Haji Fisabilillah Mosque, also known as the Cyberjaya Mosque. Raja Haji Fisabilillah ibni Daeng Chelak, a Bugis warrior from Indonesia's Penyengat Island, is the namesake of this contemporary, futuristic mosque.

Hours: Open 24 hours

Phone: 03-8322 0096

Address: Persiaran Semarak Api, Cyberjaya, 63000 Cyberjaya, Selangor
